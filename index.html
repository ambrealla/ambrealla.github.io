Hey you, yes you! Welcome to my hub. I'm Amber, in case you haven't figured it out yet.

Like most people, I do a lot of things. I cook, bake, journal, game, play, study, and work, and I found that it was impossible to have just one site contain it all. Please enjoy your stay!
